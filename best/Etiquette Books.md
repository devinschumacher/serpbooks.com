The century-old tradition of Emily Post's etiquette guidance gets a thorough update in this centennial edition, addressing everything from digital communication norms to modern social interaction expectations. Co-authored by Emily Post's great-great grandchildren, this practical guide covers the full spectrum of etiquette topics, from table manners and gift-giving to video conferencing etiquette and tipping practices in the sharing economy. Rooted in core principles of respect and consideration, the book includes handy reference tools and real-world examples to help readers navigate both familiar and novel social situations with confidence.
