![Securing the MRAP | Image](https://books.google.com/books/publisher/content/images/frontcover/HX1EEAAAQBAJ?fife=w4000-h6000&source=gbs\_api)

*Lessons Learned in Marketing and Military Procurement*

<br>

When Humvees proved vulnerable to improvised explosive devices in Iraq, the military turned to mine-resistant, ambush-protected vehicles (MRAPs) for a solution. James Hasik's "Securing the MRAP" chronicles the defense industry's years-long marketing effort to convince the Pentagon of the MRAP's value, offering crucial insights into military procurement and its impact on battlefield outcomes.
