## [Getting Illustration Clients](https://serp.ly/@serpbooks/github/amazon/Getting+Illustration+Clients?i=stripbooks)

![Getting Illustration Clients | Image](https://books.google.com/books/publisher/content/images/frontcover/adXvEAAAQBAJ?fife=w4000-h6000&source=gbs\_api)

<br>

The commissioning process for commercial illustration can be daunting for aspiring professionals, but "Getting Illustration Clients" demystifies this complex journey through the eyes of those who hire and brief freelance illustrators. Authors Jo Davies and Derek Brazell, building on their previous work "Becoming a Successful Illustrator, " provide insider tips to help illustrators navigate the global market, with full-color examples and case studies from leading companies like Harper Collins and VMLR&Y Wooga Games. This practical guide organizes key action points and summaries to support independent work, making it an essential resource for students, graduates, and early-career professionals seeking to understand the commissioning process and succeed in the commercial illustration field.
