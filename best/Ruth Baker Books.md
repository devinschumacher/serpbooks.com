Autoethnographic Tactics to Closing the Gap on Educational Attainment examines the historical and cumulative factors contributing to the education gap in underrepresented populations. Through careful analysis of stressors, structural inequalities, and social determinants, this co-edited volume presents practical strategies for improving higher education access and outcomes. The book highlights the crucial role of supportive relationships and mentorship in helping students navigate the challenges of academic life, offering both theoretical frameworks and actionable insights for educators and policymakers committed to equity and inclusion.
