## [Certain Notions of Neutrosophic Topological K-Algebras](https://serp.ly/@serpbooks/github/amazon/Certain+Notions+of+Neutrosophic+Topological+KAlgebras?i=stripbooks)

![Certain Notions of Neutrosophic Topological K-Algebras | Image](https://books.google.com/books/publisher/content/images/frontcover/HE3VDwAAQBAJ?fife=w4000-h6000&source=gbs\_api)

<br>

This research article explores the application of single-valued neutrosophic sets to K-algebras, introducing the concept of single-valued neutrosophic topological K-algebras and examining their properties. The authors investigate C5-connectedness, super connectivity, compactness, and Hausdorff conditions within this framework, as well as the behavior of these structures under homomorphism. The work extends intuitionistic fuzzy sets and contributes to the broader field of neutrosophic topology.
