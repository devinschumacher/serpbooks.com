*A Comprehensive Guide*

<br>

A meticulously crafted guide to the heart of every vehicle, "How Car Engines Work: A Comprehensive Guide" unravels the complexities of automotive powerplants from steam to electric. This authoritative book covers everything from basic engine anatomy and the four-stroke cycle to advanced fuel systems and ignition technology. Richard Man, the knowledgeable author, presents car engines as the evolutionary pinnacle of human innovation, explaining their crucial role in our mobile society while offering practical insights into maintenance and troubleshooting. Whether you're a curious beginner or a seasoned professional, this essential guide will transform your understanding of the engines that power our world on wheels.
