Smruti S Pattanaik weaves together accounts of political leaders, diplomats, and ordinary citizens whose collective actions led to the country's liberation war and ultimate independence. The book stands as a crucial resource for scholars and researchers examining the complex aftermath of Partition, offering detailed insights into the diplomatic and military strategies that defined Bangladesh's fight for freedom.
