In this powerful retelling of Cinderella, Ella returns home after her mother's tragic passing to find her father remarried. Her new stepmother and sisters torment her daily, but Ella remains steadfast in her promise to care for her father. When the crown prince returns from battle and a ball is announced, Ella longs for one last glimpse of her mother's beloved garden before leaving home. What awaits her on that arched stone bridge will change everything. This 70-page story kicks off a five-book series that reimagines fairy tales through the lens of strong, courageous women who refuse to wait for rescue. Instead of focusing on physical transformation, the series highlights the strength it takes to stand up against abuse and pursue one's own happiness.
