![Doing Political Science | Image](https://books.google.com/books/publisher/content/images/frontcover/vAScDwAAQBAJ?fife=w4000-h6000&source=gbs\_api)

*An Introduction To Political Analysis*

<br>

"Doing Political Science" provides an in-depth introduction to political analysis, exploring how researchers' theoretical assumptions influence their conclusions. The book examines five distinct paradigms to demonstrate the impact of different levels of explanation and data interpretation on our understanding of political phenomena. This 276-page text serves as an essential guide to the methodologies and frameworks that define the field of political science.

## [Contemporary Empirical Political Theory](https://serp.ly/@serpbooks/github/amazon/Contemporary+Empirical+Political+Theory?i=stripbooks)

![Contemporary Empirical Political Theory | Image](https://books.google.com/books/publisher/content/images/frontcover/smloEAAAQBAJ?fife=w4000-h6000&source=gbs\_api)

<br>

Contemporary Empirical Political Theory brings together leading political scientists to examine the major debates and recent movements in empirical political theory. Published by the University of California Press, this volume features contributions from four past APSA presidents and one IPSA president, representing theoretical orientations from behavioralism to rational choice, cultural theory to postmodernism, and feminism to Marxism. The essays address fundamental questions about human nature, cultural influence, and the objectivity of political theories, offering valuable insights into the discipline's evolution and future developments.
