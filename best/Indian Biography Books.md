*Or, an Historical Account of Those Individuals Who Have Been Distringuished Among the North American Natives As Orators, Warriors, Statesmen, and Other Remarkable Characters; Volume 2*

<br>

"Indian Biography" by Benjamin Bussey Thatcher is a scholarly compilation of biographical portraits featuring North America's most distinguished native figures across oratory, warfare, statesmanship, and cultural leadership. This well-researched work illuminates the characters and deeds of America's indigenous peoples, preserving their legacy for future generations.
