*The Emerging Role of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning*

<br>

In this forward-thinking book, forecasting experts Michael Gilliland, Len Tashman, and Udo Sglavo team up to explore how artificial intelligence and machine learning are transforming business forecasting. Through insightful case studies and practical discussions, the authors examine the impact of these technologies on everything from deep learning models to big data analytics, while 16 distinguished contributors offer additional perspectives on the future of forecasting in business.
