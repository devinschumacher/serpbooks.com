## [Studies in Archaeological Conservation](https://serp.ly/@serpbooks/github/amazon/Studies+in+Archaeological+Conservation?i=stripbooks)

![Studies in Archaeological Conservation | Image](https://books.google.com/books/publisher/content/images/frontcover/hKYGEAAAQBAJ?fife=w4000-h6000&source=gbs\_api)

<br>

Archaeological conservation transforms corroded metal, ceramic, and organic fragments into the beautiful objects that fill museum cases worldwide. This book presents 26 case studies from leading museums and heritage organizations, demonstrating the specialized techniques used to preserve and investigate archaeological artifacts. From copper alloy objects suspected of bronze disease to the delicate conservation of the Burrell Collection's ancient bronzes, these studies highlight the challenges and rewards of safeguarding our cultural heritage.

<br>

Plagues and Their Aftermath examines how societies recover from pandemics, drawing on 2500 years of history to explore the parallels between past and present. Through meticulously researched case studies ranging from ancient Athens to the 20th century, this concise book reveals the common patterns of economic devastation, social disorder, and political upheaval that follow in the wake of infectious diseases. As we face the challenges of a post-COVID world, Jenkins provides a masterful framework for understanding what kind of future awaits us, making this work essential reading for both general audiences and historians.
