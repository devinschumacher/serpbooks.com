## [The Old Boston Post Road](https://serp.ly/@serpbooks/github/amazon/The+Old+Boston+Post+Road?i=stripbooks)

![The Old Boston Post Road | Image](https://books.google.com/books/publisher/content/images/frontcover/6ic3DwAAQBAJ?fife=w4000-h6000&source=gbs\_api)

<br>

"The Old Boston Post Road" by Stephen Jenkins chronicles the history of America's first monthly mail service, established in 1673 between Boston and New York. This comprehensive account follows the development of the post road through various historical events, including its role during the French Wars and the Revolutionary War. Jenkins details how the route, which passed through significant towns like New Haven, Hartford, Springfield, and Worcester, evolved from a wilderness path to a major transportation artery connecting the Northeast to the Midwest. The book highlights key figures and locations that shaped the road's history, making it an essential read for anyone interested in early American postal history and the development of colonial transportation routes.
