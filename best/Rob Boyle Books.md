The book chronicles Boyle's major scientific contributions while also exploring his conflicted relationships with contemporaries and his own turbulent spiritual journey. Through careful analysis of Boyle's surviving papers and letters, this work offers both casual readers and history enthusiasts a fresh understanding of the scientific revolution's most influential characters.
