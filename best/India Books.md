*How China Shaped U.S.-India Relations During the Cold War*

<br>

Fateful Triangle examines how China shaped U. S. -India relations during the Cold War, revealing three decades of diplomatic evolution from 1949 to 1979. Through documents from India and the United States, Tanvi Madan illustrates how perceptions of China significantly influenced the two nations' policies toward each other, highlighting the complex interplay between shared threats and strategic differences that continue to define this crucial triangular relationship in the 21st century.
