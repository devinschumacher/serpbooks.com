## [Globalization in a Glass](https://serp.ly/@serpbooks/github/amazon/Globalization+in+a+Glass?i=stripbooks)

![Globalization in a Glass | Image](https://books.google.com/books/publisher/content/images/frontcover/s7ytEAAAQBAJ?fife=w4000-h6000&source=gbs\_api)

*The Rise of Pilsner Beer through Technology, Taste and Empire*

<br>

"Globalization in a Glass" traces the fascinating journey of Pilsner beer from its creation in 1842 to becoming the world's most popular beer style by the early 20th century. Through meticulous research spanning three continents, Malcolm F. Purinton reveals how technological innovations, imperial trade networks, and changing consumer tastes converged to transform a single German beer style into a global phenomenon. Drawing from a wealth of archival sources, this study illuminates the intertwined effects of empire, trade, and cultural exchange that shaped the modern beer industry, offering valuable insights into the broader forces of globalization that continue to influence our world today.
