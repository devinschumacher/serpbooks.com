## [Jerome Robbins](https://serp.ly/@serpbooks/github/amazon/Jerome+Robbins?i=stripbooks)

![Jerome Robbins | Image](https://books.google.com/books/publisher/content/images/frontcover/r6dvDwAAQBAJ?fife=w4000-h6000&source=gbs\_api)

<br>

This witty biography traces Jerome Robbins' transformation from a corset-making son in Weehawken, New Jersey, to a master of theater on Broadway. Born Jerome Wilson Rabinowitz in 1918, Robbins resisted his family's business and instead pursued dance, renaming himself and revolutionizing the stage. Wendy Lesser chronicles his iconic works for both ballet and theater, including "West Side Story, " "Peter Pan, " and "Fiddler on the Roof, " highlighting the Russian-Jewish immigrant's profound impact on American performance arts.
