*A Ballroom Dance Novel*

<br>

In this beautifully written novel, two talented dancers compete for the prestigious US Ballroom Championship. Nina Fortunova, a former champion now divorced and nearly thirty, teams up with the charismatic Jorge Gonzalez to reinvent the Smooth style. Meanwhile, Carly Martindale risks her reputation by dancing with the enigmatic Trey Devereux, a three-time champion returning to competition for mysterious reasons. As mentorship turns to friendship and then competition, these gifted performers must decide how far they're willing to go to win, both on and off the dance floor. Through dual narrators, "The Winner" explores the high costs of ambition, the complexities of gender roles in dance, and the timeless appeal of ballroom competition. This literary romance will captivate readers who've fallen in love with dance through shows like "Dancing with the Stars" and "Strictly Come Dancing. "
