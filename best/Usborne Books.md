## [Look Inside Our World](https://serp.ly/@serpbooks/github/amazon/Look+Inside+Our+World?i=stripbooks)

![Look Inside Our World | Image](https://books.google.com/books/publisher/content/images/frontcover/uX-qzwEACAAJ?fife=w4000-h6000&source=gbs\_api)

<br>

Travel the globe without leaving your reading nook in this interactive lift-the-flap adventure by Emily Bone. Explore over 80 flaps that reveal Earth's secrets, from the rainforest's tiniest insects to the deep sea's mysterious creatures. Along the way, discover the planet's hottest and coldest spots, and unfold a world map that brings each continent to life through its hidden surprises. This colorful journey serves as a playful introduction to geography, complete with internet links for young explorers to continue their discovery online.
